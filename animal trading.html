Louisiana Alligator

Sex is determined by climate rather than DNA. Eyes glow in the dark. Care for their young, unlike most reptiles.

  • Behavior: Hunt mostly at night
  • Habitat: Large reptiles that live in freshwater
  • Life Span: 35-50 yrs
  • Diet: Wild hogs, other gators, etc.

A fully mature gator has around 80 teeth but can go through as many as 3000 in a lifetime. Their jaws can shut with up to 2000lbs of pressure per square inch. They have been around for nearly 200 million years and there are nearly 2 million wild alligators in Louisiana!
